FAQ & Guides

Account & Security

Resetting two‑factor authentication (2FA)
  1. Go to Settings › Security.
  2. Select Reset 2FA and verify your identity.
  3. Confirm via email/SMS and set up a new authenticator.
Verifying your ID

Ensure your document is valid, well‑lit, and matches your profile details.

Payments & Cards

Why is my withdrawal pending?

Bank processing times and security checks can delay withdrawals up to 3 business days.

Card declined

Check your card limits, available balance, and that your billing address matches.

Trading & Prices

How do price alerts work?

Create an alert from the asset page; you’ll get a push/email when triggered.

Compliance & Tax

Getting a tax report

Download your transaction history from Reports and share with your tax advisor.
